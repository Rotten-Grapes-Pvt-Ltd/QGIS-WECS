# Participant Presentations and Discussion

This session provides an opportunity for participant groups to present their capstone outputs, discuss their findings, and discuss institutional applicability.

---

## 1. Group Output Presentation
Each group will present:

* The delineated watershed boundaries and calculated basin statistics (area, average slope, drainage density).

* The surface water distribution map.

* A summary of spatial datasets used and coordinate systems configured.

---

## 2. Institutional Applicability Discussion
Discussion on how these workflows can be integrated into daily operations at WECS, including maintaining database registries, standardized metadata, and automated report layouts.
