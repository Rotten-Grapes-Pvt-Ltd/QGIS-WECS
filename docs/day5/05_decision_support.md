# Geospatial Decision Support Systems

A **Geospatial Decision Support System (GDSS)** integrates spatial data with environmental models to help policymakers evaluate planning scenarios.

---

## 1. Evidence-Based Basin Management
At WECS, GDSS allows planners to:

* **Evaluate Hydropower Feasibility:** Overlaying stream network flow parameters with slope grids, transmission lines, and conservation area boundaries to find optimal project locations.

* **Map Flood Risk Zones:** Combining river hydrology models with demographic datasets to prioritize flood defense infrastructure budgets.
