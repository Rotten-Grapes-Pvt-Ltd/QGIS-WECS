# Flood and Water Resource Applications

GIS-based terrain analysis plays a critical role in flood hazard mapping, reservoir planning, and watershed prioritization.

---

## 1. Flood Inundation Modeling (HEC-RAS & GIS)
Hydrologists couple 1D/2D hydraulic models (like HEC-RAS) with GIS:

* **Preprocessing:** Extracting river cross-sections, bank lines, and flow paths from high-resolution DEMs in GIS and importing them into HEC-RAS.

* **Postprocessing:** Importing HEC-RAS water depth outputs back into GIS to map flood inundation zones and assess community vulnerability.

---

## 2. Reservoir Catchment Analysis

* **Volume Calculation:** Calculating the storage volume of a planned reservoir at different water levels using the **Surface Volume** tool in QGIS.

* **Inflow Estimation:** Modeling the catchment area draining into the reservoir site to predict seasonal inflow.
